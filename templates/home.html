CropiGo - Crop Recomendaton System



CropiGo is an ML based Crop Recomendaton system, which is a computer-based solution that uses data analytics and artificial intelligence techniques to recommend suitable crop types for a particular location or farmer.




The recommendations provided by the system can help farmers make informed decisions what to plant and when,thereby increasing crop yield and reducing the risk of crop failure. Additionally, the system can also help farmers optimize their use of resources, such as fertilizers and pesticides, by recommending the most effective inputs for a particular crop.
